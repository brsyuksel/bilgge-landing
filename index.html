app for your notes and secrets

image.
what bilgge does is nothing. instead, your browser does.

bilgge is a web application which aims you to feel that you are storing your secrets in a real safe house. what makes bilgge different is its approach on privacy and security. first, instead of saying "it uses end-to-end encryption" then asking you 8+ characters password to protect your data, it gives you power to encrypt your data by yourself in your browser. this means, bilgge servers never see your plain data, they only store your data, encrypted in your browser before sent to server. and also, bilgge avoid you leave footprints behind. in app or in this page, there is no usage of cookie, tracker, localstorage and even public cdn services.

it is completely free and open source.

you can run your own bilgge instance far away from eyes and take its control totally.

• why am I uploading my private key on login page?

actually, you are not uploading. you only gives it to your browser to use it for encryption and decryption processes. when you want to login, bilgge sends you an ecrypted random text and except you to decrypt it then send plain in order to prove "it is you". your browser decrypts to cipher text then sends it back to bilgge, and, you are in.

• how bilgge search on my content if they are encrypted?

first, bilgge does not make search, it does match. when you register bilgge, your browser generates a unique random salt data and stores it in bilgge as encrypted. the combination of this salt data and each word of your content titles are getting hashed with sha-256 algorithm by your browser. when you make a search, bilgge performs this process on your keywords then tries to match them in your data. since every user has a different salt data, there is no pattern between words that some can understand.

• why is my username not encrypted?

because of making us able to give you support. when you contact us for a problem like deleting your account, we can not match your user account if it is encrypted.

• is there a way to recover my data?

no, it is not possible. make sure the world shakes when some one figure out that it is possible. if you have lost your private key, there is only one thing that we can do is delete your wasting-some-space data.
